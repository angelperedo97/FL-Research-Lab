# Federated Learning & Intelligent Systems Research Lab

The Federated Learning & Intelligent Systems Research Lab is directed by Dr. Sergei Chuprov at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley (UTRGV). Our flagship focus is Federated Learning (FL) — with particular emphasis on personalized frameworks and optimization algorithms — and we actively pursue interdisciplinary machine learning applications across domains.

---

## Core Research Pillars

### Pillar 1: Robustness & Security in Machine Learning

We investigate adversarial vulnerabilities, model integrity, and runtime monitoring techniques to ensure machine learning systems remain reliable and trustworthy in adversarial and uncertain environments.

### Pillar 2: Privacy-Preserving & Personalized Federated Learning

Our research advances decentralized optimization methods and personalization strategies to address Non-IID data and heterogeneity across edge devices while preserving user privacy.

### Pillar 3: Intelligent Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)

We design lightweight edge models and efficient deployment strategies for smart automation and environmental monitoring in cyber-physical systems, balancing resource constraints with robust intelligence.
