Title: "A Performance Case Study of Vision-Language Models for Roadway Flood Detection Using Traffic Cameras"
Status: "Active"
Summary: "This project evaluates the performance of Vision-Language Models in detecting roadway flooding from traffic camera images. The goal is to assess their accuracy, reliability, and practical applicability for supporting real-time flood monitoring and improving public safety during extreme weather events."
