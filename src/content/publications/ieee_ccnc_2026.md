summary: "In this paper, we present a novel framework that integrates Machine Learning (ML) with Lean Construction practices to address construction industry challenges in quality management and waste reduction. While Lean Construction offers solutions, its effectiveness is often limited by manual processes such as data collection and processing. Our primary novelty is the application of ML-based methods to predict contractor performance using historical data, an approach not widely explored previously. We address this by developing a custom web application to automate the collection of audit data from a large-scale real-world oil refinery construction project spanning 586 hectares. This data was employed to train several ML models to predict contractor performance grades. Our results demonstrate the framework’s effectiveness, with the models achieving high predictive accuracy of over 85%, showing their potential to provide reliable, data-driven insights."
